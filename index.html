About Me

I am from Lawton, Oklahoma. I attended the University of Oklahoma for my undergraduate degree. I obtained a Bachelor of Science in 2010 specalizing in Multidisciplinary Studies: Technical Sciences. In that time, I began to study base elements of Solidworks and Java. This is where my love of technology and software began to grow. After I departed from the University of Oklahoma, I moved to Oklahoma City, Oklahoma to begin my career. I started in the oil & gas industry as an independent landman. This was very enjoyable, and I found the work very satisfying, as well.
I moved to Denver, Colorado in March of 2013. I made the move for a number of reasons. Most important being that I had landed a new job working for Garlock Pipeline Technologies in Wheatridge, Colorado. Currently I am working for a solar company called Blue Raven. In my spare time I enjoy spending time with my family and friends, distance running, skiing, riding my bike, experiencing live music, camping, reading, yoga, and traveling.
